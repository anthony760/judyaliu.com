Welcome

Welcome! I invite you to browse through this website. I understand that life is busy and there is a lot to do, so I made this site as focused and concise as I could. This was done to make your journey through this site as simple and easy as possible. There is information here about me, (Judy Liu), so you have an opportunity to get a sense of who I am, how I work, what my priorities are and what commitments I am making to you. Then you can determine if these aspects are compatible with your values and if they satisfy the requirements you are looking for in a Life Coach or a Therapist.

Hopefully you will relax some and even experience some nurturing as you view some of the photos interspersed with the text. In all my studies regarding the latest info on brain research and our neurology I have really learned how important our sensory experience is as a contributor to balancing our brain. When our brain is balanced then we are more balanced! Please be sure to review the “Heart Breath” as a balancing tool to quiet your mind and reduce anxiety.

I conduct workshops, in service presentations for office staff, groups as well as one to one, couple and family sessions. If you have any questions please feel free to contact me (check the contact info for direct access to me). I would be happy to arrange a free 30 minute sample session if you are interested. I have an office in Mammoth Lakes, CA at 549 Old Mammoth Road, Suite 10, and an office in North County San Diego where I see people face to face. However most of my sessions are phone sessions.

I hope you enjoy your experience here. I look forward to connecting with you soon!
